Bridging Theory & Practice

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

Our specialized model emphasizes the critical link between leading-edge academic insight—driven by our core UK university partnerships—and real-world deployment. We ensure practitioners are fully equipped to assess, govern, and mitigate tomorrow's complex digital and AI threats.
