The IDR logo employs an isometric cube framework to visually represent structure and resilience, core tenets of managing digital risk. Within the thick, structural outer lines lies a solid, vibrant orange core with distinct facets, symbolizing the dynamic and central nature of risk, AI, and digital transformation. The primary palette of deep black (#0A0A0A) and vibrant orange (#FF5A00) conveys an authoritative yet innovative identity, while clean geometric lines ensure precise legibility even at small sizes like favicons.
